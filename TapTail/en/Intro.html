App Name

TapTail

Introduction

Extremely optimized for rapid entry, minimizing the burden of tracking. Build your own cash flow statement and see exactly where your money goes.

Key features

- Privacy first – Fully offline, your data stays completely secure.

- Always ready to record – Persistent entry screen; start tracking as soon as you open the app.

- Customizable categories – Define your own income/expense types and their order for quick selection. Swipe left to edit or delete, long press to reorder.

- No data silos – One‑tap export of entries within any chosen date range.

- Built‑in statistics, with option to export to Excel for deeper analysis.

- No ads, no in-app purchases.
