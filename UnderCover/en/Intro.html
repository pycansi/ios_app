App Name

UnderCover: DIY

Description

No ads, no in-app purchases. Stable. Offline.

The classic party game. This version is highly flexible, allowing you to customize the word sets and manually select who is eliminated.

Word Configuration Guide: Use the format 'Unused,CivilianWord,UndercoverWord' or 'Used,CivilianWord,UndercoverWord'. Only words marked as 'Unused' will be selected by the game.
