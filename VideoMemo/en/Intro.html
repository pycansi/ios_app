App Name

VideoMemo

Introduction

No ads, no in-app purchases. Add notes to your videos.

Too many videos to search through? Try this!

Attach a line of text after recording a video — then easily search for the video using that text, without having to play it back.

The app is fully integrated with your system gallery. Videos recorded in this app appear in your gallery, and videos shot with your native camera can also have notes added in this app.
