App Name

AntiAA

Introduction

No ads, no in-app purchases. A multi-person settlement tool.

“AA” means splitting the total equally among all participants, while “AntiAA” means pooling the total to a single person.

The app supports 2–8 people. You can set a starting amount for each person, and then proceed with multiple rounds of distribution. When each round starts, each participant can either pay an amount or pass. At the end of the round, the total amount collected is distributed. The next round then starts automatically.
